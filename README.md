# Northwind-Traders-Dashboard

**Project Description:**
An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze and monitor the sales performance of Northwind Traders across different time periods, products, and operational metrics. The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue trends, shipping costs, sales growth, and discount impact to support data-driven business decisions.

**Key Findings :**

- July 2013 (first month) all 20 customers were new. By 2014–2015 the model shifts heavily toward returning customers (90–100% retention), meaning Northwind has a strong loyal base.
- **Beverage (21%)** and **Dairy product(19%)** contributes to most of the revenue.
- Total of **$86.90 revenue loss** has been due to discounts.
- There has been 50% increase in the Total Revenue from the last year.
- Some months has seen significant reduction in Revenue **February 2014**, **June 2014** and **November 2014** (All more than 30% reduction in revenue).
- Revenue to fright ratio shows a bad picture. The shipping price is increasing more than the revenue which is concerning.
- **Quick- Stop (9%)** and **Save-a-lot-Markets (8%)** are our top customers.
